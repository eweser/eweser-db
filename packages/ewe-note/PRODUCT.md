# Product

## Register

product

## Users

People who want an offline-first personal knowledge base that feels calm enough for daily writing but capable enough for serious note systems. They are often familiar with Obsidian-style workflows, including folders, wiki links, tags, metadata, and keyboard-heavy navigation. They use the app for capture, thinking, drafting, and long-lived personal organization.

## Product Purpose

EweNote is a local-first note app built on EweserDB. It should deliver Obsidian-class note-taking capability, including structured metadata and power-user workflows, without looking like a cluttered developer tool. Success means the note itself stays primary, advanced features stay available without dominating the screen, and the interface remains credible for sustained daily use.

## Brand Personality

Quiet, capable, precise.

The interface should feel calm, native, and editorial rather than loud or dashboard-like. It should borrow Bear's restraint and polish while supporting the depth and extensibility users expect from Obsidian.

## Anti-references

- Notion-style chrome-heavy composition where controls and metadata overwhelm the document.
- Overbuilt productivity dashboards with pills, cards, and status UI competing with the note body.
- Flashy "AI app" styling, including decorative gradients, noisy motion, or novelty controls.
- Developer-tool density that makes the app feel like a markdown IDE before it feels like a writing surface.

## Design Principles

- The note is the primary surface: content first, interface second.
- Powerful features should stay close at hand, but surface progressively.
- Metadata is supportive, not headline content.
- Calm density beats empty theatrics and beats clutter.
- Familiar note-taking patterns are good, but only when visually disciplined.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- Maintain strong readable contrast in both light and dark themes.
- Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` and avoid decorative motion.
- Keep keyboard-visible focus states across navigation and editor controls.
- Preserve discoverable non-mouse paths for writing and formatting workflows.
